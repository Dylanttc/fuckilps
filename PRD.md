## 1. Overview

### One-liner
A web app that shows Singaporeans exactly how much money they're losing to Investment-Linked Policy (ILP) fees ‚Äî and how much more they'd have if they just invested in a simple index fund.

### Why this matters
- ILPs are one of Singapore's most widely sold financial products, yet most policyholders don't understand the compounding drag of annual fees (typically 1.5‚Äì3.5% p.a.)
- Financial advisors earn recurring commissions from ILPs, creating misaligned incentives
- No free, easy-to-use tool exists that lets a consumer quantify the actual cost of their ILP in dollar terms
- This is a **public good** project ‚Äî empowering financial literacy

### Target user
